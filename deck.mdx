## SoLiD - Discussion

---

<div style="display: flex; flex-direction: row; margin-bottom: 20px;">


###### Technology

- How to make more ordinary people use SoLiD?
- What's the use of Verifyable Claim? Can it replace block chains?
- Can in-company data governance benefit from BaaS?
- Can we implement Semantic Web or Web 3.0 without RDF?
- How do you see the similarities and differences between W3C's Web 3.0 and Blockchain's Web 3.0?
- How to implement the Web 3.0 envisaged by Tim?
- How to make Web 3.0 technology development popular?
- How to associate, verify and trace the public data vs. private data vs. circulating data in blockchain?
- What are the application scenarios of zero-knowledge proof technology in SoLiD?

###### Commercialization

- What are the viable business models or stories in the direction of privacy/security/personal data?
- What are the potential benefits of promoting SoLiD for companies?
- What difficulties will SoLiD encounter in landing in China? How to solve these difficulties?
- What are the viable business models for SoLiD Pod providers?
- How to design a roadmap to promote SoLiD's landing in China?
- How do you think Blockstack implements many of SoLiD's ideas? Does it make sense to do similar projects in China?
- Will China have laws similar to GDPR?

</div>


###### Views

- Which privacy should be exposed to /public and which should only be authorized to individual applications?
- Will SoLiD subtly change people's perception of privacy?
- What do blockchain practitioners think of SoLiD?
- How to associate "computing" with "privacy protection"?

---

<div style="display: flex; flex-direction: row; margin-bottom: 20px;">

###### 技术

- 如何让更多的普通人用上 SoLiD？
- Verifyable Claim 到底有什么用，能不能取代区块链？
- 现在的公司内数据治理能不能从 BaaS 上受益？
- 实现语义网或 Web3.0 能不能不用 RDF？
- 如何看待 W3C 的 Web 3.0 和区块链的 Web 3.0 之间的异同？
- 如何实现 Tim 设想的 Web 3.0？
- 如何使 Web 3.0 技术开发变得平民化？
- 区块链 链上公开数据 vs. 链下隐私数据 vs. 流通数据，如何关联，如何验证，如何溯源？
- 零知识证明技术在 SoLiD 中有哪些应用场景？

###### 商业化

- 在隐私/安全/个人数据这个方向上，有什么可行的商业模式或故事?
- 推动 SoLiD 对大家的公司都有什么潜在的好处？
- SoLiD 在中国落地会遇到哪些困难？如何解决这些困难？
- SoLiD Pod 提供商有哪些可行的商业模式？
- 如何设计一个路线图，用以推动 SoLiD 在中国落地？
- 如何看待 Blockstack 实现了 SoLiD 的很多设想，在中国做类似的项目还有意义吗？
- 中国会有类似 GDPR 的法律吗？

</div>


###### 看法

- 哪些隐私应该公开到 /public，哪些应该只授权给个别应用？
- SoLiD 会潜移默化地改变大家对隐私的认知吗
- 区块链从业者是怎么看待 SoLiD 的？
- 如何把「计算」与「隐私保护」关联起来，「隐私保护」是一个比较主观的词
